REQUIRED AUDIO FILES
====================

Place the following MP3 files in this folder:

1. amazon_jungle.mp3
   - Duration: at least 60 seconds (will loop)
   - Content: Amazon rainforest ambience — birds, insects, rain, river
   - Free sources:
       • Freesound.org — search "amazon jungle ambience loop"
         (e.g., user "felix.blume" has high-quality jungle recordings)
       • Zapsplat.com — search "rainforest ambience"
       • BBC Sound Effects archive: sound-effects.bbcrewind.co.uk

2. city_bogota.mp3
   - Duration: at least 60 seconds (will loop)
   - Content: Urban / city street ambience — traffic, people, market sounds
   - Free sources:
       • Freesound.org — search "city street ambience loop"
       • Zapsplat.com — search "city traffic ambience"
       • OpenGameArt.org — search "ambient city"

TIPS:
- Keep each file under 3 MB for fast mobile loading.
- Use MP3 at 128 kbps (stereo) — good balance of quality vs size.
- Normalize audio to -14 LUFS to avoid volume shock when the card opens.
- Ensure files are loopable (beginning and end should crossfade naturally).
