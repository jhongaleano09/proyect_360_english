REQUIRED IMAGE FILES
====================

Place the following equirectangular 360° images in this folder:

1. amazon_360.jpg
   - Resolution recommended: 4096 × 2048 px (minimum 2048 × 1024)
   - Content: Amazon jungle / rainforest environment
   - Free sources:
       • Poly Haven (polyhaven.com/hdris) — search "forest" or "jungle"
       • Unsplash: unsplash.com (search "equirectangular forest")
       • NASA Visible Earth for atmosphere panoramas

2. bogota_360.jpg
   - Resolution recommended: 4096 × 2048 px
   - Content: Colonial street / old building courtyard
   - Free sources:
       • Poly Haven — search "city" or "courtyard"
       • Unsplash: search "equirectangular city street"
       • Google Street View exports (via Street View Download 360 Chrome extension)

TIPS:
- Keep file size under 4 MB each for mobile performance.
- Use JPG at 85% quality (not PNG) to reduce load time.
- Verify the image is truly equirectangular (2:1 aspect ratio).
